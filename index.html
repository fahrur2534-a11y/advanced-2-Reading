Global Environmental Challenges
Instruction:
Read the text carefully and answer all questions below.

Text

Global environmental challenges have become one of the most serious issues in the modern world. Problems such as climate change, air pollution, deforestation, and plastic waste continue to increase due to human activities.

Climate change is mainly caused by greenhouse gases such as carbon dioxide. These gases trap heat in the atmosphere and lead to rising global temperatures.

Air pollution is mostly caused by vehicle emissions and industrial activities. It can lead to respiratory diseases.

Deforestation reduces oxygen production and destroys habitats for many species.

Plastic waste remains in the environment for hundreds of years and harms marine life.

Solutions include renewable energy, recycling, planting trees, and environmental education.
